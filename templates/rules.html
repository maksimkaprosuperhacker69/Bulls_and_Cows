Game Rules

Objective

Bulls and Cows is a code-breaking game where two players participate. The first player thinks of a sequence of 4 distinct digits, and the second player must guess it.

How to Play

  • The computer (or first player) selects a secret sequence of 4 distinct digits (default: 4 digits)
  • You (the second player) sequentially make guesses by entering your own variant of four digits
  • After each guess, you receive feedback in the form of "Bulls" and "Cows"

Understanding the Feedback

Bull: A digit that is in the correct position in the sequence.

Cow: A digit that is present in the hidden sequence, but is not in the correct position.

Example

Hidden sequence: 3904

Your guess: 0324

  • Bulls: 1 - The digit "4" is in the correct position (fourth position)
  • Cows: 2 - The digits "0" and "3" are present in the hidden sequence but in wrong positions

Game Modes

  • Guess Mode: You try to guess the computer's secret sequence of digits. The computer provides feedback (bulls and cows) after each guess. The game finishes when you guess the sequence correctly (4 bulls).
  • Secret Mode: You think of a sequence of digits, and the computer tries to guess it. You provide the feedback (bulls and cows) after each computer guess. The game finishes when the computer receives the answer "4 bulls".

Tips

  • Start with guesses that cover different digits to gather information about which digits are in the sequence
  • Use the bulls to identify correct positions
  • Use the cows to identify which digits are in the sequence but need repositioning
  • Eliminate possibilities systematically based on the feedback
  • Remember: the sequence consists of distinct digits (no repetitions)

Winning

You win in Guess Mode when you guess the secret sequence correctly (4 bulls). In Secret Mode, the computer wins when it correctly guesses your sequence (receives 4 bulls).
